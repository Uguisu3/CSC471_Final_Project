Babylon 5 Defender

John Nightingale - CSC 471 - Fall 2018 - Christian Eckhardt

Project Description

Babylon 5 Defender is a 3D space shooter game based on the TV show Babylon5. The goal of the game is to protect the Babylon 5 station from the shadow vessels (enemy ships) that are shooting at the station. This is done by flying a Starfury shooting at the shadow vessels.

To win the game get the number of shadow vessels displayed in the top right corner of the screen to 0 before Babylon 5 loses all of it's health
